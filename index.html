About the Project

Welcome to ZAYUVALYA - AI Humanizer, a powerful paraphrasing tool designed to humanize text effortlessly. This tool is completely free, open-source, and requires no login. It’s built for writers, students, and anyone who needs text rephrased while preserving its meaning and natural tone.

Our tool uses a robust synonym-based engine that considers the context of your input, ensuring that essential terms and names remain unchanged. Highlighted changes in the output make it easy to identify modifications.

Currently in development, ZAYUVALYA - AI Humanizer aims to be your go-to text humanizing assistant!

How to Use

  1. Enter your text in the input box on the left.
  2. Click the Paraphrase button to see the humanized text appear on the right.
  3. Words replaced are highlighted for easy identification.
  4. Use the Copy Output button to copy the results.
  5. Track word and sentence counts below the input and output boxes.
